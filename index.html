FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does it take to see results?

Typically, you'll see initial engagement improvements within 2-4 weeks, with significant growth occurring over 3-6 months of consistent strategy implementation.

Which social media platforms do you work with?

I specialize in Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube. The platform mix is determined based on your target audience and business goals.

Do you provide content creation services?

Yes, I offer comprehensive content creation including photography, videography, graphic design, and copywriting services.

How do you measure success?

Success is measured through key metrics including follower growth, engagement rates, reach, website traffic, and ultimately, business conversions and sales.

What's included in your monthly packages?

Monthly packages include strategy development, content creation, community management, analytics reporting, and ongoing optimization based on performance data.
